Restoring Fletcher High's Legacy

Being visionary and competent is part and parcel of leardership as Ophrah Whitney once said,

The worst part of being blind is having sight but no vision at all.
The Students Leadership Board's vision is Restoring Fletcher High's Legacy. Moreover, every year Fletcher High School has a mandatorial role of making history and establishing students in order for them to be academically matured. The best possible way to restore Fletcher High School's legacy is by giving substance to the motto and significance of the number one uniform. The motto that reads, ,
Per Scientium Ad Virtutem
which means, Throuh Knowledge to Virtue or moral goodness. The number one uniform signifies that we are greater than the greatest and brighter than the brightest, and above all, the brain masters the world is yearning for.

Whenever we put on the number one uniform, w scrutinise that leadership is a challenge to be more than just average.

UNITED WE STAND. DIVIDED WE FALL.
